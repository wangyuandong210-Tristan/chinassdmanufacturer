About ORION SSD & Memory

Your trusted partner in SSD and memory manufacturing since 2013

Our Story

Leading China SSD Manufacturer

Founded in August 2016 in Shenzhen, China, ORION SSD & Memory has grown from a small memory module workshop to a comprehensive storage manufacturer with an 10,000m2 factory, 200+ employees, and annual production capacity of over 10 million units.

We specialize in the R&D, manufacturing, and OEM/ODM of SSDs (NVMe & SATA), memory modules (DDR4/DDR5), and industrial storage solutions. Our products are exported to 50+ countries across North America, Europe, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East.

Our mission is to provide global B2B partners with high-quality, cost-effective storage solutions backed by reliable manufacturing and responsive support.

Timeline
Our Journey

Company Milestones

2013 - Entered the SSD and memory module industry. Started with memory module assembly.
2016 - ORION SSD & Memory formally established. First OEM partnership signed with a European brand.
2020 - Moved to 10,000m2 factory. ISO 9001 certified. Exported to 20+ countries.
2022 - Launched NVMe SSD line. Reached 30M units cumulative production.
2024 - DDR5 production line launched. 30+ export countries. 200+ B2B partners.
2025 - 30M+ units shipped. New industrial SSD line. Enterprise storage solutions.
