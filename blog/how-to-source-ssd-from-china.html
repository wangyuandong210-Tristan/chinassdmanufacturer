How to Source SSDs from China: A Complete B2B Buyer's Guide 2025

June 28, 2025 · 12 min read

China is the world's largest manufacturer of SSDs, producing over 60% of global supply. For B2B buyers looking to source SSDs for private labeling, distribution, or system integration, understanding the landscape is essential.

Why Source SSDs from China?

China's SSD manufacturing ecosystem offers significant advantages for B2B buyers, including factory-direct pricing 30-50% lower than branded products, flexible OEM/ODM services with custom branding and low MOQ, a complete supply chain with all components available locally, fast production lead times of 15-30 days, and full certification support (CE, FCC, ROHS, REACH).

Step 1: Define Your Requirements

Before contacting suppliers, clearly define your product specifications including capacity (256GB to 2TB), interface (NVMe PCIe 3.0/4.0 or SATA III), NAND type (TLC for balanced performance, QLC for budget, SLC/MLC for industrial), quantity (MOQ typically 50-200 pcs), and required certifications (CE/FCC for EU/US, ROHS/REACH for EU).

Step 2: Find Reliable Manufacturers

The best Chinese SSD manufacturers are concentrated in Shenzhen. When evaluating suppliers, verify their manufacturing capability - do they own a factory or just trade? Check their NAND sourcing partners (Samsung, Micron, Kioxia, or YMTC), quality control processes including burn-in testing, certification validity, and client references from similar markets.

Step 3: Request Samples

Always request samples before placing a bulk order. Test read/write speed consistency using CrystalDiskMark, monitor temperature under sustained load, verify compatibility with Intel and AMD platforms, and inspect PCB quality and solder joints physically.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical MOQ for OEM SSD orders from China?

Standard OEM orders typically start at 100-200 units per SKU. Some manufacturers offer lower MOQ for trial orders at higher per-unit pricing.

How long does OEM production take?

Sample production takes 7-15 days. Mass production takes 15-30 days depending on quantity and complexity. Factor in 3-7 days for shipping via DHL or FedEx.

Can I get my own brand on the SSDs?

Yes. OEM manufacturers provide custom labeling on the drive, custom retail packaging, and custom firmware with your brand name displayed as the drive model in operating systems.

What certifications are needed to import SSDs?

For EU: CE and ROHS. For US: FCC. For UK: UKCA. Most Chinese OEM manufacturers include these certifications as part of the OEM service.
