How to Choose an SSD OEM Manufacturing Partner in China

July 10, 2025 · 10 min read

Choosing the right SSD OEM manufacturing partner in China is one of the most important decisions for your storage brand. Here is what every B2B buyer should evaluate before signing a partnership.

Key Evaluation Criteria

CriteriaWhat to Look ForRed Flags
Factory OwnershipOwn production facility, not a trading companyRefuses factory visit or video call
NAND SourcingUses Samsung, Micron, Kioxia, or YMTC branded NANDCannot name NAND source or uses generic flash
PCB QualityUses black 10-layer PCB for better signal integrityCheap green PCB with visible defects
CertificationsCE, FCC, ROHS, REACH, ISO 9001Claims certifications but cannot provide certificates
Quality Control100% burn-in testing, performance verificationSample-only testing, bulk shipment not tested
MOQ FlexibilityOffers trial orders at manageable volumesRequires 1000+ units for first order

Questions to Ask Before Partnering

  • Can you provide a factory video tour? A genuine manufacturer will show you their SMT lines, QC lab, and packaging area.
  • What NAND flash brands do you use? Reputable factories use branded NAND from major manufacturers.
  • What is your defect rate? Industry standard is under 1%. Quality-focused factories track and share this data.
  • Can I visit the factory? Most reliable partners in Shenzhen welcome buyer visits.

The Importance of OEM Communication

A good OEM partner communicates clearly and regularly. Look for factories with English-speaking sales teams who provide regular production updates, respond to inquiries within 24 hours, and provide sample approval processes before mass production.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify a Chinese SSD factory?

Request a video call tour, ask for certification copies, request client references, and check if they appear on Alibaba Verified or ThomasNet.

What certifications are needed for SSD import?

CE for EU, FCC for US, ROHS for EU. Most Chinese OEM factories include these.
