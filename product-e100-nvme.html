ORION E100 NVMe SSD

High-performance NVMe SSD for OEM and industrial applications

Product Overview

The ORION E100 NVMe SSD delivers reliable high-speed storage for demanding applications. Built with a black 10-layer PCB and premium TLC NAND flash, it offers excellent performance for both consumer and industrial use cases.

Available for OEM/ODM with custom branding, packaging, and firmware configuration.

Key Features

  • PCIe 4.0 x4 NVMe interface for sequential reads up to 7,000 MB/s
  • Available in 256GB, 512GB, 1TB, and 2TB capacities
  • Black 10-layer PCB for superior signal integrity and reliability
  • TLC NAND flash with DRAM cache buffer
  • M.2 2280 form factor for broad compatibility
  • 3-year warranty with OEM support options
  • Custom branding, labeling, and packaging available
